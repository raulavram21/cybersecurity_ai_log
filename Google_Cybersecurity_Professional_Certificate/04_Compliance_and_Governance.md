Compliance and Governance


Data Classification

    PII (Personally Identifiable Information): Data that can identify an individual (e.g., name, phone number, IP address).

    SPII (Sensitive PII): A highly restricted subset of PII (e.g., Social Security Numbers, medical records, biometrics). (Context: Requires much stricter handling guidelines due to severe legal and financial penalties if breached.)

Regulatory Frameworks

    Compliance: The process of adhering to internal standards and external regulations to avoid fines and breaches.

    The EU AI Act: A 2026 framework categorizing AI systems by risk tiers. (Context: Mandates strict transparency and synthetic content watermarking for generative AI, forcing companies to adopt AI Governance as a core security pillar.)
