# Hi, I'm Raul.

I'm moving into cybersecurity and AI from an extensive background in financial
crime, where I learned how bad actors think, move money, and exploit
weaknesses in systems and in people. What drives me is finding the weakness others miss, the flaw in a system
or a process that an attacker would exploit. As more of that system becomes
AI, the weak points move too, and that is the edge I want to understand:
where security and intelligent machines meet.

### Where my interest sits
I'm keeping my scope wide while I learn. Right now I lean toward offensive
work, red teaming and bug bounty, where you probe a system the way a real
attacker would. I'm just as curious about threat intelligence, the blue
team side of defense, and how AI is reshaping both how attacks happen and
how we stop them. 

### What I bring with me
An investigator's mindset: pattern spotting, building a case from messy
evidence, and explaining risk to people who are not technical. I'm pairing
that with the technical foundations, the tooling, and the practice that
security work demands.

### What I'm working on
Real, verifiable skill. The Google Cybersecurity Certificate and CompTIA
Security+ for foundations, growing comfort in the terminal, applying investigative tools, and learning how AI fits into modern security.

### What you'll find here
A working record of that journey. Study notes in my own words,
scripts as my Python knoweldge grows, and write ups that show how I think, not just
what I can recite.
